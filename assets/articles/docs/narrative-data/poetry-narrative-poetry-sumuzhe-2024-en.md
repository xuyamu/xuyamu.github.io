
*(Composed for Erlang, the Sacred Divinity, a character featured in Black Myth: Wukong)*

**蘇幕遮·梅見峰**
**Su Mu Zhe · Mei-Jian Peak**

梅山寒，雪滿窗。浮屠塔外，眾生又蒼茫。 
Cold grips the slopes of Mount Mei, snow blankets the pane; Beyond the Pagoda, the boundless world is lost in a vast and grey domain.

治御權主多無義。天命無情，難容自由心。 
The ruling sovereigns hold no justice, cold and hollow within; Heaven’s Mandate is ruthless, brooking no free heart that dares to sin.

念故人，追往昔。見峰山頂，孤影映寒光。
 Yearning for an old friend, I trace the shadows of yore; Atop Meijian Peak, a lone silhouette glimmers in the frost’s cold core.

心知傲骨猶存世。無言承重，終有重逢日。 
Deep within, I know the unyielding spirit survives the years; Bearing the burden in silence, until the day our reunion finally nears.
