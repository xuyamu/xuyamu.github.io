**空山知己**
**A Kindred Spirit in the Empty Mountain**

空山清兰悄然开，
In the silent hills, a pure orchid blooms in solitude,

幽谷自有暗香来。
From the deep vale, a hidden fragrance drifts through the quietude.

不与众芳争暄妍，
It seeks no rivalry with common blooms for showy grace,

独钟乾坤风雨声。
Communing only with the winds and rains in heaven’s embrace.
